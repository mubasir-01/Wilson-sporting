About Wilson Sporting Goods

Founded in 1914, Wilson Sporting Goods has been a pioneer in providing top-tier sports equipment for athletes around the world. With a rich legacy of innovation and excellence, we are committed to supporting athletes in their pursuit of greatness.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: At Wilson Sporting Goods, we are dedicated to developing high-performance gear that allows athletes to perform at their very best, no matter their sport.

Vision: Our vision is to inspire the next generation of athletes through advanced technology, superior craftsmanship, and a commitment to sustainability.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation: Continuously evolving to bring the latest technologies to our products.
  • Quality: Providing the highest quality products to help athletes achieve peak performance.
  • Sustainability: Creating eco-friendly products that respect the environment.
  • Integrity: Operating with honesty and responsibility in every aspect of our business.
  • Community: Supporting sports communities through initiatives and sponsorships.
